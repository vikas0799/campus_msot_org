## Centralized Ecosystem Highlights (Home Page Subsections)

### 1. Clubs & Communities
Explore technical and campus societies, algorithmic groups, and creative circles:
* **Featured:** NEXCELL Coding Cell, Tech Club, Sports Cell, Cultural Society.

### 2. Blogs & Newsletters
A markdown publication workspace for technical write-ups, placement diaries, interview prep guides, and campus weekly newsletters.

### 3. Placements & Achievements
Dynamic job and internship directories including startup hiring, remote listings, referral opportunities, and student placement achievement highlights.

### 4. Students Portfolio
A professional showcase displaying student skillsets, certifications, custom project lists, resume downloads, and synced coding profile ranks.

### 5. Campus Leadership
Connect with campus student ambassadors, faculty steering coordinators, and placement convenors.

### 6. Alumni Network
A network bridge linking current students with graduates for career advice, mentorship, and referral pipelines.

### 7. Mirai Operation HQ
* **Location:** Gurugram, Haryana
* **Details:** The operational headquarters overseeing platform development, server management, and centralized campus collaborations.

### 8. Contact US
Get in touch with campus ambassador cells, faculty boards, or technical administrators.

---

## Benefits for MSOT
* **Structured coding culture** across all campuses.
* **Better placements** with direct referral databases.
* **Strong college branding** and centralized visibility.

## Route Maps Reference

### Public Pages
* **Homepage Hub:** `/`
* **Campus Dashboards:** `/[campus]` (e.g. `/ghaziabad`, `/jaipur`, `/bangalore`)
* **Campus Societies:** `/[campus]/clubs/[club_slug]` (e.g. `/ghaziabad/clubs/nexcell`)
* **Campus Coding Tables:** `/[campus]/leaderboard/[platform]` (e.g. `/ghaziabad/leaderboard/leetcode`)
* **Global Placements:** `/opportunities`
* **Global Coding Rankings:** `/leaderboard`
* **Technical Roadmap:** `/communities`
* **Markdown Publications:** `/blogs`
* **Blog Creator:** `/blogs/create`
* **Read Article:** `/blogs/detail/[slug]`
* **Student Portfolios:** `/profile/[username]`
* **Auth:** `/login` & `/register`

### Central Admin Panel (Protected)
* **Analytics Console:** `/admin`
* **Dynamic Layout Cards CRUD:** `/admin/cards`
* **Manage Events:** `/admin/events`
* **Moderate Publications:** `/admin/blogs`
* **Configure Clubs:** `/admin/clubs`
* **Post Placements:** `/admin/opportunities`
* **Allocate User Roles (Super Admin):** `/admin/users`
